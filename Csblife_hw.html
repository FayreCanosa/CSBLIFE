10 Things aboout Jean-Baptiste de La Salle

1.) He is considered the founder of the first Catholic schools.

2.) At na early age of 16, he was named canon of Rheims Cathedral.

3.) He began school for the poor at his hometown together with Adrian Nyel; Institute of the Brother of Crhistian School.

4.) He dedicated himself for the service of the children.

5.) he De La Salle Brothers were the first Roman Catholic religious teaching order that did not include any priests.

6.) De La Salle was a pioneer in programs for training lay teachers.

7.) De La Salle founded what is generally considered the first normal school; that is, a school whose purpose is to train teachers.

8.) He died on Good Friday.

9.) He was the patron of teachers.

10.) He continues to educate children even he had no enough money.
